Tacos recipe

with this recipe you will be abble to make some incredible suadero tacos.

Suadero is meat with some chunks of fat that make's the meat juicer than other types of meet

Ingredients

  • Suadero (1 k)
  • Tortillas (1/2 k)
  • Salt
  • Fat
  • 1 Onion
  • Celery

How to do it

  1. Put some fat on a previously heated pan, when the fat gets melted, put the suadero on the pan
  2. add some salt to the suadero and keep cooking it until the meat is soft
  3. one the suadero is cooked, put it of the pan and chop it in to small pices of meet
  4. shop the celery and the onion
  5. heat up some tortillas and put some suadero meet on them
  6. add some celary and onion to your tacos

ENJOY!
